Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions
tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your ner favorite holiday start !

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500mg) all-purpose flour
  • 1 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the toppin:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounches brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necesessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh on frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attacchment,mix toghete all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorded. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead the dough, though I Sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
